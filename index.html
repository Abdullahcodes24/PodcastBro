What it's all about?

So, a lot of you guys might be wondering what this page is all about. Well, I can tell you what it is about. So, back in the days when I was feeling genuinely shit all the time, I figured out a way that helped me improve. And the weird thing about it, I didn't even notice it that I was gradually improving. The people around me were the ones, who saw improvement in me and told me about how much I had chaged in past couple of months.

Yeah, I am talking about podcasts. In my opinion, podcasts are a great way of storytelling. In a frame where, it feels more like being engaged in a conversation other than, listening to a straight up story. So, there's a real-life touch in it and the best thing about it is that you'll never know which turn the show takes next. So, you get hooked up till the very end.

How is it related to self-improvement and all that shit? Well, that's the neat part. You see, in podcasts (not talking about lame jerky, useless kind of podcasts in which hosts invite strippers and talk about things which have no meaning or good impact on one's life) there are ideas being exchanged at a constant pace between the host, the guest (or just the host if it's a solo podcast) and the listener(s). These ideas may hold a lot of value and have the potential to bring a change or improve the life of the viewer/listener.

I won't say, it was "the only way" through which I was able to improve but it definitely helped me into reshaping my mindset and I took some steps which allowed me to inspect my inner-self and toggled introspection in me. Because of such steps, I was able to achieve some of the greatest milestones of my life! For instance, I wrote a book at the age of 18, by being encouraged by intellects online through podcasts. I mean, Bro! I still can't believe it. It usually take years for other people to write a book and get that book published, but I was able to do it in just 22 days.

Again, it might be the only way you could become a better version of yourself, but I will try my best to provide you with something that could potentially add value to your life, and hopefully help you improve.
